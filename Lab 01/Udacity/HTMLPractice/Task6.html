HObbit

2017 was a landmark year not only for Hobbit, but also for the entire CIS community. The news of Gambit's victory at PGL Major Krakow 2017 spread all over the world[1], and the winnings were $ 500,000. After a while, the players began to leave the organization and in September 2018, Hobbit was put up for transfer. After leaving, he returns to his colleagues under the K23 tag and wins 1,500,000 tenge at WESG 2018 Central Asia: Kazakhstan. However, three months later he joins the European organization HellRaisers.
