Travel to Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is an amazing country that can surprise with its beauty and diversity. Traveling around Kazakhstan is an unforgettable experience that will leave a lot of vivid impressions.

Astana

The first place worth visiting in Kazakhstan is its capital, Astana. This is a city that has undergone huge changes in recent years and has become a real center of modern architecture. Here you can see such famous sights as Bayterek, the Palace of Peace and Accord, as well as many other interesting places.

Almaty

Next, you should go to Almaty, the cultural and economic center of Kazakhstan. Here you can visit many museums, theaters, galleries and other cultural sites. It is also worth visiting the Alatau mountains, where you can enjoy the most beautiful landscapes and spend time outdoors.

Burabay

If you love nature, then you should go to the Burabay National Park, which is 200 km from Almaty. Here you can see unique species of vegetation and animals, as well as enjoy the beautiful mountain scenery.
